My name is Callum, and in the summer of 2021, I am embarking on the greatest motoring adventure on the planet, with my two mates, Jack and Alberto.


We are going to drive our (t)rusty Fiat Panda from Oxfordshire (home of Blenheim Palace, Didcot Railway Centre, and the Uffington White Horse) to Mongolia (home of the Mongolian Plateau, the Altai Mountains, and some camels).

This 10,000 mile trip has been an annual event since 2004, raising money for Cool Earth ever since it's first rally with only six teams. Since then, it has grown to around 300 teams every year, and in 2021, that cohort includes us for the first time.

10,000 miles!

We need your help!


There are two areas we could use some assistance:

1. Fundraising for Cool Earth.

Easy peasy, donate some money and help us support rainforest communities to halt deforestation and its impact on climate change.

2. Sponsorship for the journey.

Want to help us reach the finish line?

 

There are loads of ways of doing this, maybe you would like your shiny company logo displayed proudly on our bonnet for all to see, an in return you have some products or services that might help us?

 

Or maybe you have some old jerry cans lying around, which you meant to throw out three years ago, but they might just stop us from getting stranded in the middle of bumf*ck nowhere? Let us know with the email below!

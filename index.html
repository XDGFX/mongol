Hi! I'm Callum, a design engineering student from the University of Bath, and in the summer of 2021, I am embarking on the greatest motoring adventure on the planet - The Mongol Rally, alongside my two mates, Jack and Alberto.


This entails driving our (t)rusty Â£400 Fiat Panda from Oxfordshire (home of Blenheim Palace, Didcot Railway Centre, and the Uffington White Horse) all the way to Mongolia (home of the Mongolian Plateau, the Altai Mountains, and some camels).

This 10,000 mile endeavour has been an annual event since 2004, raising money for Cool Earth ever since it's first rally with only six teams. Since then, it has grown to around 300 teams every year, and in 2021, that cohort includes us for the first time.

10,000 miles!

But, to do this, we need your help!

1. Fundraising for Cool Earth.

Easy peasy, donate some money and help us support rainforest communities to halt deforestation and its impact on climate change.

We really appreciate any help, even if it's as little as slipping our journey into a conversation with your family dog-walker, and sliding them the link to our website. Thank you!

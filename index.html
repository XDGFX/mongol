I'm Callum Morrison, a design engineering graduate from the University of Bath, and in the summer of 2021 2022 (thanks to COVID), I am embarking on the greatest motoring adventure on the planet * - The Mongol Rally, alongside my two mates, Jack van Galen and Alberto Guerra Martinuzzi.

* second only to the M25 at rush hour.


This entails driving our (t)rusty 1.2 litre lunchbox from Oxford, UK all the way to Mongolia (home of the Mongolian Plateau, the Altai Mountains, and some camels).

This 10,000 mile endeavour has been an annual event since 2004, raising money for Cool Earth ever since it's first rally with only six teams. Since then, it has grown to around 300 teams every year, and in 2021, that cohort includes us for the first time.

10,000 miles!

But, to do this, we need your help


There are two areas you can get involved:



1. Fundraising for Cool Earth.

Cool Earth is the official charity of the Mongol Rally. They work alongside rainforest communities to halt deforestation, helping to reduce it's impact on climate change.

 

Tropical forests are among the world's most carbon-dense forests, due to their incredible capacity for carbon sequestration (capturing and storing carbon dioxide). Protecting them is one of the most effective ways we can tackle climate change.

2. Sponsorship for the journey.

Want to help us reach the finish line?

 

There are loads of ways of doing this, maybe you would like your shiny company logo displayed proudly on our bonnet for all to see, and in return you have some products or services that might help us?
